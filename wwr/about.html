History

Rapid Rafting Origins was founded in 1995 by outdoor enthusiasts Jake and Maria Thompson, who envisioned a sustainable adventure company that offered thrilling white-water experiences. Starting with just a few inflatable rafts and a passion for nature, the company initially operated on the scenic rivers of the Pacific Northwest. Rapid Rafting quickly gained a reputation for its commitment to safety, environmental stewardship, and exceptional customer service.

Over the years, it expanded its operations to include guided excursions, eco-tours, and team-building retreats. By incorporating environmentally friendly practices and collaborating with local communities, Rapid Rafting Origins has grown into a premier rafting company, attracting adventure seekers while preserving the natural beauty of the rivers it traverses. Today, it remains a staple in adventure tourism.
